Our Story

Made with Passion

Chinese King Restaurant, located in the vibrant Ashok Nagar locality of Balabhaupeth, Nagpur, is your go-to destination for delicious and authentic Chinese food. With a stellar rating of 4.2 out of 5 from our loyal customers, we take pride in serving mouthwatering dishes that bring the true taste of Chinese cuisine to Maharashtra. Whether you're craving a quick lunch or a satisfying dinner, our kitchen is open every day from 1:00 PM to 9:00 PM to serve you the best.

At Chinese King Restaurant, we believe great food brings people together. Our menu is thoughtfully curated to offer a wide variety of classic Chinese preparations using fresh ingredients and bold flavors. From sizzling noodles and fried rice to flavourful soups and starters, every dish is prepared with care and consistency.
