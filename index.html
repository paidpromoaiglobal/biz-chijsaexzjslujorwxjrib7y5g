Our Story

Anu's Boutique and Dresses Shop

Anu's Boutique and Dresses Shop is a trusted clothing store nestled in the heart of Mappedu, Poonamallee, Chennai. We take pride in offering a wide range of stylish, comfortable, and affordable clothing for men, women, and children, catering to the diverse fashion needs of our community. Our collections blend contemporary trends with timeless elegance, ensuring every customer walks out looking and feeling their best.

Located conveniently on MS Dhoni Street, we have built a loyal customer base through our commitment to quality, personalized service, and competitive pricing. Whether you are shopping for festive sarees, everyday kurtis, kids wear, or blouse materials, Anu's Boutique and Dresses Shop is your one-stop destination for fashion that speaks to your style and budget. Visit us today and experience the difference that passion for fashion makes.

  • 5-Star Rated Boutique – Loved and trusted by our local community in Poonamallee
  • Affordable Pricing – Premium quality clothing and fabrics at budget-friendly prices for every family
  • Wide Collection – Sarees, kurtis, kids wear, and blouse materials all under one roof
